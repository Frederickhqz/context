# Product Context & Core Concepts (2026)

## The Philosophy: "Beats over Notes"
In the Context app, we move beyond the concept of a "Note" as a dead piece of text. We view information as a dynamic **Mesh of Beats**.

### 1. Atomic Meaning (The "Beat")
A **Beat** is the smallest indivisible unit of meaning. When you write a note, the AI (Apple Intelligence, Gemini Nano, or WebLLM) dissects it into:
- **Characters:** Entities with agency.
- **Conflict:** Opposing forces.
- **Themes:** Abstract recurring concepts.
- **Events:** Specific happenings in time.

### 2. The Multi-Dimensional Mesh
Beats do not exist in isolation. They are connected via relationships:
- **Mirrors:** Parallel patterns (e.g., a modern event reflecting a historical one).
- **Causes:** Direct causal chains.
- **Contradicts:** Narrative inconsistencies or logical paradoxes.
- **Evolves To:** Transformations of concepts over time.

## Technical Architecture

### 1. Unified Intelligence Bridge
To ensure your "Universe" looks the same on every device, we use a **Shared Native AI Bridge**.
- **Locked Embedding:** `nomic-embed-text-v1.5` (768 dimensions).
- **Native Implementation:** Foundation Models framework (iOS) and AICore (Android).

### 2. Gravitational Visualization (Universe)
The 3D Universe uses a hierarchical physics engine:
- **Suns:** High-frequency Core Concepts (4+ connections).
- **Planets:** Primary beats orbiting concepts.
- **Moons:** Granular details orbiting primary beats.
- **Asteroids:** Orphaned or isolated ideas in the outer belt.

### 3. Narrative Coherence (Contradiction Engine)
The system automatically detects when you describe something in a way that conflicts with prior entries.
- **Valence Detection:** Detects emotional shifts (e.g., character described as "saintly" vs "malevolent").
- **State Conflict:** Detects physical impossibilities (e.g., character in two places at once).
