# Context Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name:** Context  
**Tagline:** Your second brain for notes, ideas, and connections.

### Brand Personality
- **Clever** - Intelligent without being pretentious
- **Clean** - Minimal, focused, distraction-free
- **Connected** - Everything links to everything
- **Curious** - Encourages exploration and discovery

### Logo Concept
The Context logo represents:
1. **Nodes** - Your notes, ideas, and knowledge
2. **Connections** - Links between concepts
3. **Context** - The surrounding framework that gives meaning

### Logo Variants

#### Primary Logo
- Full "Context" wordmark with icon
- Use on light backgrounds

#### Secondary Logo  
- Icon only
- Use when space is limited

#### Inverse Logo
- White/light on dark backgrounds
- Same proportions

### Logo Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "C" in Context on all sides.

### Logo Don'ts
- Don't stretch or distort
- Don't change colors outside brand palette
- Don't add shadows or effects
- Don't rotate
- Don't place on busy backgrounds

### App Icon
- iOS: Rounded square with gradient background
- Android: Circular icon
- Web: Favicon with simplified mark

### Writing Style
- **Clear** - Use simple, direct language
- **Conversational** - Write like you're explaining to a friend
- **Action-oriented** - Use verbs over nouns
- **Concise** - Remove unnecessary words
